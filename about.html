About Retneddy

Retneddy is a registered Consultancy Company located in Airport North Road, Embakasi Area, Nairobi. We deal in in all Airport Services and all Immigration Services in Kenya. The services range from e-government services (Visas, Permits and Passes) application, Hotel Bookings, Airport Transfers, Tour Guiding, Car Rental Services etc. Our consultants are selected from highly experienced industry professionals covering a vast range of expert areas. Consultants are selected from a pool of specialist who can provide and match the exact nature of the consultancy.
