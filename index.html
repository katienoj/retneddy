10 Tips for a successful Travel

Book On-line: Booking your airport taxi on-line is the best way to ensure you have a record of the booking.

Information: Give us much detail about your travel arrangements as possible.

Check your dates: It’s easy to make a mistake.

Use a meet and greet service

Turn on your phone : Providing your mobile number is an excellent way of allowing the company to get in touch.

Let us help: Carrying your bags, minding your luggage or just pointing you in the right direction should all be part of the service.

Don’t be afraid to ask: If you would like anything during your journey to and from the airport, just ask.

Feedback: Don’t forget to let the company know what you thought of the service you received.

Enjoy the service: Finally, sit back and relax.
